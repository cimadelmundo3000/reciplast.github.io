ABOUT US
Reciplast de Venezuela C.A.
We are a private company dedicated to logistic services for the import and export of strategic material, susceptible to recycling. We currently have a payroll of ninety-four (94) employees.
Meet Us !
What do we do ?
We carry out export services monthly, mobilizing loads of approximately thirty thousand metric tons (30,000tm) of ferrous material and five hundred metric tons (500tm) of non-ferrous materials.
MISION

We are a company dedicated to providing logistics services for import and export, storage, transportation, both land, sea and air, for the distribution and marketing of plastic products for all types of applications, as well as industrial recovery, used oils, to optimize international trade activity, in order to further improve the handling services for full containers and loose cargo of strategic material in Puerto Cabello in Carabobo State, including comprehensive logistics for the development of shipping operations of load.

Oriented to satisfy the current and future needs of our customers; innovating, generating value and profitability for the economic and social development of the nation, committed to the well-being of our workers, the conservation of the environment, under the principles and organizational values.
Vision
To be a national reference company, internationally recognized in logistic services for import and export, storage, transport of strategic material, susceptible to recycling, profitable and innovative.
